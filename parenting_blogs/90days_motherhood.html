90 days of motherhood

Mother, the most beautiful and compassionate call in the world. And how much every mother has to suffer to hear this small but wonderful call. Today I will discuss some things to do in the first three months of motherhood. Hope it will be of some benefit to new mothers.

After nine long months in the mother's womb, the little baby is not at all mentally prepared when he sees the face of the world. That's why he starts screaming and crying as soon as he comes into the world and this crying continues for several months after birth.

Acknowledge the child's behavior and engage with him understanding that this is his only way of expressing himself. This crying of the baby is a normal process but in some cases this crying can be excessive. There could be several reasons for this. For example, some babies have colic. In that case, the baby may cry continuously for several hours. The child has this problem until the age of 3 to 4 months and then it gradually gets better.

There are also some babies who start crying at a certain time and that too continues for quite some time. I heard from my mother that for the first three months of my birth, I used to cry continuously all night and it would continue until dawn. When I was a child, I used to think it was impossible but now I know it is really possible and I salute all the mothers who bear this impossible hardship and raise their children.

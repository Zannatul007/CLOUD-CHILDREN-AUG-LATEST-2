10 Foods That Help Children Grow Taller

All parents want their children to grow tall and healthy. But do we know which foods help children grow taller? Here are 10 foods that help increase height in children.

  • Eggs:

    Eggs are the nutritious food that plays the most important role in increasing the height of the child. So, the child should be given one egg a day. Having fun with fried or boiled eggs for breakfast can be a great way to start the day and get protein in your body.

  • Milk:

    Milk contains calcium which helps in bone growth and makes bones strong. Protein is also related to milk. Give the baby 1 mug of milk every day or make fun food with milk.

  • Soyabeans:

    Soybean contains a lot of protein which helps in muscle and bone growth.

  • Oatmeal:

    Oatmeal is a very protein-rich food and is low in fat. Oatmeal is a healthy food for people of any age. Oatmeal can be given to the child once a day.

  • Chicken:

    Children love any food made with chicken. Chicken meat is very healthy and rich in protein.

  • Vegetables:

    Greens contain iron and calcium. Both these elements are helpful in increasing the height of the child.

  • Carrot:

    Carrots are rich in vitamin A, which helps in protein synthesis in the body. Raw carrots contain more vitamins than cooked carrots. So, you can give the baby carrot salad or juice.

  • Fruits:

    The role of fruits for the good health of children is more or less known to everyone. Fruits rich in vitamin A like mangoes, watermelons, tomatoes help increase the height of the baby and make the bones strong.

  • Grains:

    Wheat-based bread-pasta, red rice khichuri, wheat cereal- these contain vitamin-B and iron which makes the baby taller.

  • Yogurt:

    It contains vitamin D and iron which are essential for the baby. If the child does not like yogurt, then you can give cheese. Cheese also contains vitamin D, iron and protein. The above foods will not only help the baby grow taller, but the baby will also grow up healthy.

Diaper 24 Hours

Diapers are an essential product for babies these days. Parents want to keep babies in diapers for most of the day for their child's and their own convenience. But have you thought about how healthy it is to wear diapers for a long time?

First let's talk about the benefits of diapers. Keeping the baby in diapers reduces the hassle of cleaning the diaper. Where there is no possibility of tapering. By doing this, parents are very sure. Some babies are used to being in diapers 24 hours a day. This is especially common for children from foreign countries. This reduces the suffering of those who manage children at home. So many benefits of using diapers. But have you ever wondered what the baby's problems can be as a result of using diapers all the time?
Many may say that if children in foreign countries are wearing diapers all the time, what is the problem with wearing our children? Actually, we have no problem. But the weather of our country is quite different from the weather of foreign countries. Due to excessive heat and humidity in our country, wearing diapers all the time can cause various harm to the baby. Most babies with sensitive skin develop rashes due to regular diapers. Being wet all the time and not getting any air at all causes the problem of skin rash. Due to which the child suffers a lot, and cries.

In addition, various fragrances and chemicals are used to create fragrances in diapers or to enhance their effectiveness. Many children are allergic to these chemicals. As a result, rash may cause skin spots. The normal blood circulation of the baby's skin is disrupted due to wearing diapers that are too tight. As a result, your baby's normal growth is damaged without your knowledge and your baby suffers but cannot speak. If the baby wears diapers with feces and urine for a long time, there is a possibility of getting a urine infection. So the diaper should be changed as soon as the baby poops.

Also, it is not healthy to wear the same diaper for more than 3 hours. Change your baby's diaper every 3 hours. Always try to use good quality diapers for baby. Clean the area with a cotton pad dipped in lukewarm water or rinse with water every time you change a diaper. Then refrain from diapering him for a while. If you are in the habit of wearing diapers overnight, at least check and change them during the night or better not. If you develop a rash for any reason, use a good quality rash cream. First of all, take care of the health of the child. If the inconvenience outweighs the benefits of using a diaper, then it is better not to wear it.
